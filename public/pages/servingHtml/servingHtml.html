Nodemon

WHen do we use nodemon?

Nodemon is a smart tool, when creating code. For every change in your script, it saves the script, stops the program and then reruns the program. It makes the work progress very smooth and intuitive.

Forexample when you create a website, you don't have to go to the terminal each and every time when you make a change to your program. So simple hands on testing, is a much quicker process.
